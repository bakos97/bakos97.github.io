About Me

// the short version

I'm a fullstack engineer who builds and ships products end-to-end. I've launched multiple web apps from zero — handling everything from database design to deployment on Fly.io and cloud infrastructure.

My background is from DTU (Technical University of Denmark), where I studied data science, machine learning, and cognitive modelling. That gave me deep experience with AI/ML pipelines — from training neural networks to building data-driven systems that produce real results.

I use AI tools daily — Cursor, Claude Code, and LLM APIs are core parts of my workflow. I'm pragmatic: I find solutions with what's available and I ship. I thrive in small teams where things move fast and everyone owns what they build.

About Me

// who I am and what drives me

I studied at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU), where I built a foundation in data science, machine learning, and software engineering. But what really drives me is building things people use.

I've shipped multiple products — from web apps with active users, to mobile apps I use daily, to platforms designed for real business problems. Each solution has been tested with real users, iterated on based on feedback, and refined until it works.

I care about the full loop: identifying a problem, building a solution, putting it in front of people, and making it better based on what they tell me.

02

SaaS MVP · Employee Retention

Employee Recognition Platform

Built for companies with high turnover rates, especially those with remote workers who can feel invisible. The platform tracks employee birthdays, work milestones, and key moments — then gathers personal messages and feedback from teammates.

It generates a personalized celebration website for each employee, where colleagues' messages are displayed and gift cards can be attached. The goal: make every employee feel genuinely seen and valued.

03

Mobile App · React Native

Gold Habit Tracker

A personal habit tracking app built in React Native with a twist: a gold-based reward and punishment system. Miss a workout? You have to buy gold. Hit a goal? You get to sell some. The stakes make it stick.

Features a Kanban board for managing goals and tasks, a workout logger for tracking sessions, and the gold economy that turns discipline into a game. Built for myself, used daily.

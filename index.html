About Me

A quick snapshot of who I am and what drives me.

I'm a student at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) with a passion for data science, software engineering, and making sense of the world through numbers. My work sits at the intersection of analytics and engineering — whether that means building interactive visualisations, training models, or shipping production code.

Outside of coursework, I enjoy working on projects that have real societal relevance — like exploring gender disparities in income, education, and parental leave across Denmark.

When I'm not coding you'll find me exploring Copenhagen by bike, following football, or experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.
